Frequently Asked Questions

What is the check-in procedure?

If you are attending SD Hacks, Espresso will be on the second floor of RIMAC arena. Feel free to stop by anytime on Saturday, October 26, from 10am-3pm. If you are not attending but want to stop by you can access the second floor using the road next to Hops & Salt.

Who can attend Espresso?

All UCSD students are welcome, along with all SD Hacks attendees. No registration required!

What is the dress code for the event?

Casual attire is recommended.
